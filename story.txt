Harry potter, a young wizard, is a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The story is about Harry's fight against Lord Voldemort. He finally defeated him and became the hero of the wizard world. Now, Harry has decided to complete his education at the University of Wisconsin at Madison. He is here for his first day of school and made a new friend, Bucky B. Bucky is a very spunky and energetic badger. He is also very good at calculus and is helping harry with his homework nearly every night. What a great friend bucky has been so far. Harry was tired of college and decided to move to magical world again.
